# The UN Theory / Broken Zero — SSOT v1

## Overview
The UN Theory / Broken Zero explores the origin of states in the universe as a progression from an initial UN state (non-being, null) to the first 1 state (being, ON). 
It formalizes the rules that emerge at this transition and tracks how these rules lead to the formation of complex structures, platforms, and observers.

---

## Core Transition Sequence
1. **UN (null)** — No state, no rules, no form.
2. **Transition to 1 (ON)** — First instance of being. Rules are instantiated here.
3. **Binary Capacity** — From ON, OFF becomes possible, giving states: ON (1), OFF (0).
4. **State Interactions** — 1 and 0 interact to form sequences: 10, 01, 11, 00.
5. **Sequences → Higher Patterns** — Bitstrings combine into more complex forms.

---

## Finite Rules at Transition
1. **Persistence Rule** — ON can persist without collapsing back to UN.
2. **Absence Rule** — OFF can persist without collapsing back to UN.
3. **Toggle Rule** — State can change between ON and OFF.
4. **Replication Rule** — ON can create another ON.
5. **Interaction Rule** — ON and OFF can interact.
6. **Combination Rule** — Sequences can combine into higher-order patterns.
7. **Constraint Rule** — All changes occur within finite rules that prevent collapse to UN.
8. **Expansion Rule** — Given time, sequences tend toward greater complexity.

---

## Principles

### 1. Form–Rule Disparity Principle
The form at later stages of a system may have no apparent resemblance to its initial form, yet both are governed by the same finite rule set created at transition.

### 2. Transformed Bit Hypothesis
Light is the first transformed bit — the first allowable transformation that made observation possible. 
It is not “energy added later” but the initial manifestation of a bit in a visible form.

### 3. Gravity Emergence with Matter
Gravity arises with the transformation of bits into mass-carrying configurations. 
Gravity is the first relational force and, in effect, operates faster than light in shaping structure.

### 4. Formed Aggregate Platform Principle
The interaction of a stable existential META and a transformative, replenishing MODAL yields a formed aggregate platform capable of hosting higher-order functions, including life and observation.

### 5. Observer Shaping by META
META (existential domain) does not just define where an event occurs; it shapes the capacities and nature of the observer itself.

## Genesis Functional Mapping (Non-theological)
- **Void → Light** — First transformed bit (`LIT`), observational platform.
- **Light/Dark Separation** — Binary logic appears (1 vs 0).
- **Domain Separation** — META emerges (distinct operational zones).
- **Formation of Earth/Water Archetypes** — Stable domain (Earth) + adaptive medium (Water).
- **Platform Emergence** — Formed aggregates capable of supporting complex interactions, including life.

---

## Earth/Water Archetypes in oMMP Terms
- **META (Earth)** — Existential base condition (stability).
- **MODAL (Water)** — Transformative replenishment (adaptability).
- **PLATFORM** — Resultant formed aggregate capable of hosting higher-order functions.

---

## Constraint vs Expansion
- **Constraint** — Prevents collapse into UN by limiting state changes to allowable rules.
- **Expansion** — Enables complexity growth within constraints.
